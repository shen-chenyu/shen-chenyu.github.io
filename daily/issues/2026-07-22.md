### [Logical Judgments Under Pressure: Diagnosing Syllogistic Stability with Learned Soft Prefixes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18228v1)
**Authors:** Brian K Chen
**Published:** 2026-07-20
**Summary:** Learned soft prefixes can override correct logical judgments in LLMs, revealing model-specific stability limits.

**What's new**
- Soft prefixes redirect correct answers in 72-90% of cases across models, outperforming random controls by 37-99 percentage points.
- Dominant effect is a broad answer preference, not fixed-symbol forcing or transferable logical operations.
- Qwen models' flip predictability differs from Gemma's, highlighting model-specific logical stability.

### [Automated Discovery Has No Universally Superior Harness](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18235v1)
**Authors:** Akshat Gupta, Jermaine Lei, Alexander Lu, Gopala Anumanchipalli, Leshem Choshen
**Published:** 2026-07-20
**Summary:** No single automated discovery harness universally outperforms others; harness choice should be treated as a problem-specific hyperparameter.

**What's new**
- Decomposes OpenEvolve and TTT-Discover into components, evaluating 30 harnesses across 12 model-problem pairs with 3.1M+ LLM rollouts.
- Finds no fixed harness is reliably superior; OpenEvolve variants often underperform simpler alternatives.
- Shows early discovery progress predicts final performance, enabling adaptive allocation that prunes weak runs and outperforms fixed harnesses.

### [Machine Learning Potential-Driven Molecular Dynamics Simulations of Dehydrogenation in Pristine and Doped MgH$_2$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18182v1)

### [OR Else: A Differentiable Trust Region for Policy Optimization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18163v1)
**Authors:** Chinmay Rane, Kanishka Tyagi, Michael Manry
**Published:** 2026-07-20
**Summary:** This paper proposes replacing the clipped surrogate objective in PPO/GRPO with a smooth 'Output Reset' (OR) squared-margin loss for LLM post-training.

**What's new**
- OR introduces a smooth one-sided saturation rule, avoiding abrupt derivative changes in the objective.
- PPO-OR achieves a mean final reward-model score 0.305 higher than PPO-clip under GAE.
- GRPO-OR shows smaller spread and near-zero terminal OR residual, but no mean reward gain at group size 2.
- OR changes optimization behavior differently under GAE vs. group-relative advantages.

### [Sobek: Streaming Equivariant Tensor Product Convolutions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18074v1)
**Authors:** Vladimir Chorošajev, Cédric Bény
**Published:** 2026-07-20
**Summary:** Sobek reformulates equivariant tensor-product convolutions to avoid edge-sized intermediates, achieving up to 49.7x speedup and 99% memory reduction.

**What's new**
- Reassociates radial projection, spherical-harmonic coupling, and graph aggregation to eliminate edge-local workspace.
- Streaming formulation consumes edge products directly into bounded receiver-side state, preserving full multiplicity mixing.
- Implements forward, backward, and double backward passes with generated CUDA code.
- Outperforms OpenEquivariance in all 75 capacity-matched comparisons, with speedups from 1.2x to 49.7x.
- Executes workloads up to 100x larger than prior capacity while maintaining near-peak GPU throughput.

### [How Does Alignment Tuning Shape Representations of Sycophancy and Related Cue-Induced Biases in LLMs?](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18114v1)
**Authors:** Prakhar Gupta, Terry Jingchen Zhang, Florent Draye, Bernhard Schölkopf, Zhijing Jin
**Published:** 2026-07-20
**Summary:** Alignment tuning, not pretraining, installs distinct, steerable directions in LLMs that cause sycophancy and cue-induced biases.

**What's new**
- Bias directions are extracted from hidden states and validated via probing, transfer, and causal intervention.
- Pretrained base models show no cue-specific bias signals; alignment tuning is the primary source.
- Each bias forms a single coherent direction that can be decoded and steered to recover unbiased answers.
- Cross-bias entanglement is model-specific; behaviorally similar biases occupy distinct directions.
- Steering along bias directions serves as a modest debiasing tool, recovering errors while preserving correct answers.

### [DFT+U+V is equivalent to DFT+U with density-dependent hybridized projectors](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18071v1)
**Authors:** Edward Linscott, Alberto Carta, Nicola Marzari
**Published:** 2026-07-20
**Summary:** DFT+U+V is shown to be equivalent to DFT+U with density-dependent hybridized projectors, clarifying the role of inter-site corrections.

**What's new**
- Inter-site +V corrections are equivalent to on-site DFT+U with hybridized projectors to first order in V/U.

- Results sharpen questions on computing V and defining Hubbard subspaces.
